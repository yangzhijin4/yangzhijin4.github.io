(Mar. 2018 - Present)

  • Supervised by Dr. Haichuan Ren

Device-Free Wireless Localization and Activity Recognition

(Sep. 2017 - Feb. 2018)

  • Supervised by Dr. Yangjie Cao and Dr. Cong Yang
  • We employ physical layer Channel State Information(CSI) as the indicator of activities. Using multiple transmitter-receiver links to achieve omnidirectional personnel detection near the receiver, and the LSTM network structure to train the data set.
  • I contribute to set up the experimental environment and preprocess and train data.

A Climbing Robot for Detecting Bridge Crack

(Mar. 2017 - Sep. 2017)

  • Supervised by Dr. Yangjie Cao
  • We build a hardware and software system, the design of the robot inspired by the inchworm. We construct a two-legged multi-joint robot with three gaits(twist, reverse, and inchworm gait). And there is a cloud platform to receive the collected data in real time.
  • I participate in robot assembly and control, the communication between the upper computer and the cloud platform.

We designed two robots and applied for China’s invention patents, patent nos: 201710388616.4 and 201810138547.6.

The design details of the mechanical claw part are shown in the following figure(I draw them). The method shown in the figure adopts some new plastic materials, which can increase the friction of the underlying surface.

Highway Pavement Cracks Detection

(Feb. 2016 - Jun. 2016)

  • Supervised by Dr. Yangjie Cao
  • We cut out a section of road video taken from a highway maintenance vehicle and identified cracks in the pavement. Using the GPS data corresponding to the time where the frame with cracks, locate the crack in the freeway.
  • I participate in the preprocessing of training samples and test samples and generated a cascaded classifier.
