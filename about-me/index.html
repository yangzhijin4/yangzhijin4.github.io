Advantages

  • I’m a excellent leader.
    From my sophomore year, I started as the team leader because I was the only girl in the team. I must do some extra work, but I also have some unexpected gains such as the ability to express and teamwork. The most important thing is that I realized that the team’s cohesion is precious, which determines the efficiency of the work and the final result.

  • I have strong academic skills.
    As an undergraduate I didn’t have the opportunity to delve into some techniques more deeply, but I developed good scientific research habits from past research experiences. I believe that these qualities will make me easier to my future research career.

  • I’m a peerless girl.
    Although there are more boys here, some people think that boys are always better than girls. I don’t think this is no sense. For example, in the modeling contest, we only have four days to complete all the work. I think the girl’s thinking mode can sometimes help us solve problems better, and my physical strength is not worse than that of boys (I didn’t even sleep for one minute in the last two days).

Disadvantages

  • I don’t have a high GPA(about 3.3/4.0).
    My freshman year was the most confusing period for me. I joined the lab and the student union at the same time. Obviously, I was busy on both sides, so my grades were not very good this year. However, I chose to stay in the lab after the end of the student union duty. I know that the past can’t be changed, but it is worth mentioning that my grades have been on the rise.
